Our eco-friendly cleaning approach uses products and methods that minimize environmental impact. We use non-toxic, biodegradable cleaning solutions, reduce water waste, and follow practices that maintain excellent cleaning results while protecting both your health and the planet.

Patriotic sourcing refers to our commitment to purchasing supplies from American businesses, with emphasis on local and regional vendors. We use a tiered system that prioritizes purchases from businesses in our immediate community first, followed by state, regional, and finally national suppliers. This approach keeps money in the local economy and reduces our carbon footprint.
